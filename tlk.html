The Last Knight

The Last knight is a game that I am making by myself. This includes the coding, models, level design etc.

The Last Knight (TLK) is a first person tower defense game set in a medieval fantasy world. You must defend a castle from oncoming waves of enemies. If either you or the castle loses all its HP, you lose and the game ends.

Combat

The main method of combat in TLK is with a sword and a bow. The player can swap between these two at any point. The sword can be used at all times, however has limited range. The bow can defeat targets from a range, but requires arrows to shoot. Once you run out of arrows, you cannot use your bow until you acquire more arrows.

Crafting

In TLK, you can craft certain items when you interact with a workbench. This is how you replenish your arrow count. You can also upgrade your weapons to make them stronger if you have the required items.
